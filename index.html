Virtual Rival 2k18

Virtual Rival 2k18 is the first ever inter university gaming competition in Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka which will be organized with key idea of focused on gaming skills, planning, the efficient manner of resources usage and the team work of undergraduates as they are the next level of generation that willing to take the future and the economy of Sri Lanka.

This event is planned as the gaming encounter where university students all over the country are eligible to apply for participation and among them 16 teams will be selected for competition. It must has 5 members for each team with only one extra reserve member. After the selection all the 16 teams will be grouped under 4 groups by the organizing team. The competition will consist of several events and also there will be several games. Participant must not skilled only with one game and he/she must play several games to place their group to the next level.

Whole event will be judged by a team of technological experts and skilled gamers.
